Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipie has been in my family for generations and never fails to deligt.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp grounded cinnamon
  • six medium sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 oF (190 oC).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
